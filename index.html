ABOUT US

Organic foods are grown without artificial pesticides, fertilizers, or herbicides. Organic meat, eggs, and dairy products are obtained from animals that are fed natural feed and not given hormones or antibiotics. Natural foods are free of synthetic or artificial ingredients or additives.Pure does not mean Organic. By law Organic products must be certified by the USDA. Only 100% Certified Organic products can bear the USDA Organic Seal on the Label. To be CERTIFIED all ingredients must meet or GO ABOVE & BEYOND the USDA's STRICT STANDARDS.All Aloha Organic Fruit is 100% certified organic: peaches, apricots, nectarines, cherries, plums, pears and apples. You can enjoy safe, delicious organic fruit year-round with our jams and fruit in a jar.Products labeled as “100% organic” must contain only organically produced ingredients and processing aids, excluding water and salt. No other ingredients or additives are permitted. Products labeled “organic” must contain at least 95% organically produced ingredients (excluding water and salt).
